GlobeOfGeek Article Analyzer 1.2 

How Does This Work?

Just drag and drop the writing you wish to analyze in the box below. A variety of statistics from your writing will then appear!
 

Current Features of Program:

  1.  How much you will get paid/earn for submitting the writing (Feature applies to GlobeOfGeek members)
  2.  Displays the amount of Author-Rank credits you will earn (Feature applies to GlobeOfGeek members)
  3.  Number of Characters/Letters in article with spaces counted
  4.  Number of Characters/Letters in writing without spaces counted
